Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my password?

You can change your password using the form above. Just enter your new password and confirm it, then click "Save Changes".

Is my information secure?

Yes, we take security very seriously. All data is transmitted over a secure connection and passwords are encrypted in our database.

What happens when I toggle dark mode?

Toggling dark mode changes the color scheme of the interface to a darker theme, which can be easier on the eyes in low-light environments. Your preference is saved in your browser for your next visit.
